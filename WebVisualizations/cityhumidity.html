City Latitude vs. Humidity

Seen within this graph is the reflection of humidity and geographical location. Such as with max tempature, it can be seen that the Southern Hemisphere tends to be most affected by higher humidity ratings, where as the opposite side is not as frequently impacted by high humidity numbers.

It must be taken into consideration the time in which this data collected. Based off of the maximum tempature data, we can draw a correlation between the warmer weather and the higher counts of humidity in the Southern Hemisphere. While there seems to have been a signifigant amount of mositure, there was also warm air which increased the chances of humidity counts.
